Privacy Policy

How WhaleX handles user details and submissions.

Information collected

WhaleX may collect name, email, phone number, TradingView username, payment reference, support messages, screenshots, reviews, and uploaded files.

How information is used

This information is used to process access requests, verify payment, provide support, manage customer submissions, and improve WhaleX services.

Payments

Payments may be handled by third-party providers such as Razorpay. This website does not store card, UPI, or banking credentials.

Public content

Reviews and screenshots appear publicly only after WhaleX verification. Payment proof and access details are not shown publicly.
